## 1) Why This Story

This story supports both organic authority and AI-search citations by publishing a data-backed, methodology-transparent asset tied to high-intent career queries.

## 2) Core Narrative

- Risk is uneven across role clusters, not uniformly distributed.
- Resume readiness and role-fit gaps are measurable and actionable.
- Users improve outcomes when diagnosis precedes rewriting and application scaling.

## 3) Candidate Headline Angles

1. Which Job Families Show the Highest AI Displacement Risk in 2026?
2. The Most-Missed Resume Signals by Role Type: What Applicants Overlook
3. Why Generic Resume Advice Fails: A Role-Level Data View

## 4) Data Scope and Method

### Data source

- Aggregated, anonymized tool outputs from AISkillScore flows.
- No personally identifiable information included.

### Core dimensions

- Role family (engineering, marketing, operations, creative, finance, healthcare)
- Displacement risk band
- Resume/JD fit gap categories
- Commonly missing skill or impact signals

### Method notes

1. Group outputs by role cluster and normalize sample counts.
2. Compute frequency of top missing signals per cluster.
3. Compare gap patterns against displacement bands.
4. Publish only statistically stable aggregates.
