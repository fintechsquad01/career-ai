# Pain Intent Keyword Map

Version: 1.0  
Owner: Growth + Product + Content  
Last updated: 2026-02-20  
Review cadence: Monthly

Audience: Growth, Content, Product Marketing  
Purpose: Turn persona pain points into repeatable keyword and copy decisions.

## 1) How To Use

For any content request:

1. Pick a primary persona
2. Pick funnel stage (awareness, consideration, decision, retention)
3. Use the mapped pain and objections
4. Build copy using recommended proof and CTA style
5. Select one primary keyword cluster and 2-4 support variants

## 3) Funnel-Stage Keyword Strategy

### Awareness

- Focus: problem education and diagnostic curiosity
- Examples:
  - `why am i not getting interviews`
  - `is ATS blocking my resume`
  - `is AI replacing my job`

### Consideration

- Focus: evaluation and comparison
- Examples:
  - `best ai resume optimizer`
  - `jobscan alternatives`
  - `teal alternatives 2026`

### Decision

- Focus: cost, trust, and immediacy
- Examples:
  - `pay as you go career tools`
  - `resume optimization without subscription`
  - `how many tokens do i need for job applications`

### Retention / Expansion

- Focus: deeper utility and outcomes
- Examples:
  - `salary negotiation script generator`
  - `interview follow up answer practice`
  - `career roadmap with weekly plan`

## 4) Copy Guardrails By Intent

- Diagnostic intent: prioritize clarity and risk framing
- Comparison intent: prioritize explicit capability differences
- Pricing intent: prioritize fairness, transparency, and top-up continuity
- Action intent: prioritize concrete step-by-step instructions

## 5) Language To Avoid

- Overclaims: "guaranteed interview," "land any job"
- Price-only language: "cheapest," "budget hack"
- Vague hype: "AI magic"
- Off-brand terms: "credits," "points"
