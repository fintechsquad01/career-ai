# Growth W1-W2 Messaging Alignment Audit

Owner: Growth + Product  
Branch: `feat/growth-messaging-alignment-w1w2`  
Scope: pricing/paywall and growth-facing message consistency

## Audit Standards

- Canonical brand/entity terms are consistent (`AISkillScore`, `AI-powered career intelligence platform`, `tokens`).
- No overclaims or certainty language for probabilistic outcomes.
- Claim -> mechanism -> evidence appears in persuasive content.
- Outcome/trust framing appears before price framing.
- Shortfall math and token safety language are explicit for monetization copy.
